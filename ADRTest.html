ADR Tank Testing

We now offer ADR certified B3 Hydraulic tank testing.

Current legislation states that any tank engaging in internation transit (i.e Irish cross-border travel) must conform to ADR B3 specifications. We offer the complete service, including the following:


  • Hydraulic structural test on each compartment, witnessed by a representative from a certified competent body. (Aviva, RSA, Bureau Veritas)

  • Full leakproofness test with all equipment in place.

  • Material thickness test at required points on vessel.

Call today to book your ADR certified test.
