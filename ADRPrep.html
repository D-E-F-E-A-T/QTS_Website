ADR Preparation


We offer a full ADR preparation service. We will inspect your vehicle to ensure it complies with all current ADR regulations, and carry out any modifications necessary to pass the test. For imported vehicles this is a must to ensure your vehicle complies with all Irish regulations.

Example modifications needed:
  • Cab heat shielding
  • ADR-compliant wiring throughout the vehicle
  • ADR-compliant components such as mud wings
  • Cab-activated battery cutoff

Call today to organise your ADR preparation.
